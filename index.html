Research

I am interested in all things ISM. I use the GHOSDT (Galaxy HydrOdynamical simulations of Supernoa Driven Turbulence) simulations to investigate the cold ISM, star-formation self-regulation, and to predict emission lines.

About Me

I live a second life as a rock climber. I have toured North America living out of a minivan climbing some big walls and sometimes equally challenging small rock, from Tutokanula (El Capitan) in Yosemite Valley to Mount Hooker in the Wind River Range.
